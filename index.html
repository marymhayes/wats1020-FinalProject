Alice Johnson

Alice Johnson is originally from Seattle and came to New York to get her degree in English from Fordham University. She also studied Acting at The London Dramatic Academy, and has been living and acting in NYC for the past decade(ish). Alice works with many emerging companies, such as Stable Cable Lab Co., Blowout Theater Co., ESPA Primary Stages, and Boomerang, to name a few. You may have seen her at IRT, 59E59 Theaters, The Wild Project, Dixon Place, and many other theaters in the NYC area. She's also a member of BYG Woof Comedy Collective, and a creator of the tri-annual new short play reading series ThatThing. Alice lives in Brooklyn with her wonderful boyfriend and is an avid Theater Maker and Eater of Bagels.

    That Thing

    That Thing! is a reading series created and produced by Ashley Grombol, Alice Johnson, and Molly Garber. It was the result of a friendship that formed in an acting class in the spring of 2013. The trio wanted to create a forum for new short plays that wasn't necessarily tied to a theatre company. It allowed budding and established playwrights to hear new work aloud with professional actors in a casual setting. This was made possible by the generosity and support of Linda and her staff at Characters Bar and Grill.
